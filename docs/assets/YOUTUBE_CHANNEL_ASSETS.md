## 1. Channel Description (paste into YouTube About section)

---

TradeGame teaches trading process through a gamer lens. We cover crypto, stocks, and forex — all three treated as first-class markets.

This is not a channel about making money. This is a channel about process: how to size a position, how to journal a trade, how to review a replay, how to follow a rule when it's inconvenient.

We build an education game called TradeGame — a trading-practice simulator. No real money. No signals. No hype. Just practice, debrief, and repeat.

**Education, not financial advice.** Trading involves risk of significant loss. Simulated results are not real trading. No signals, ever.
