## 1. Service Description — Education Only

TradeGame provides an educational trading-simulation game, community resources, and
voluntary coaching programs (collectively, the "Service"). The Service is designed to
teach trading process: market structure, position sizing discipline, journaling
practice, risk management frameworks, and scenario analysis.

**Three-prong defensive posture (RISK_REGISTER §1):**

1. The Service provides process education only. No content, coaching session, game
   scenario, or community resource constitutes advice about whether, when, or how
   much capital to deploy in any security, commodity, currency pair, or crypto asset.

2. No fiduciary relationship, advisory relationship, investment-adviser relationship,
   or commodity-trading-adviser relationship is created between TradeGame, its coaches,
   or any community member and any user of the Service.

3. Coach volunteers are unpaid volunteers providing process instruction under a code
   of conduct. They are not employees, agents, registered investment advisers,
   registered commodity trading advisers, or financial planners. Nothing a coach says
   constitutes financial advice.

**The simulation is not the market.** Simulation results do not predict real-trading
outcomes. Past simulation performance does not indicate future results in live markets.
The Service contains no real-money functionality. No real brokerage accounts are
connected, accessed, or managed through the Service.

## 3. Simulation Disclaimer

The trading simulation is a **practice environment only.**

- All market data in the simulation is synthetic and generated for educational purposes.
  It does not represent real market prices, conditions, or opportunities.
- Simulation account balances, gains, and losses are fictional and have no monetary
  value whatsoever.
- A user who performs well in simulation has demonstrated process discipline in a
  practice context. This does not mean they are ready to trade real money. Live markets
  involve factors — emotional pressure, liquidity, slippage, counterparty risk — that
  a simulation cannot fully replicate.
- The Service will display "practice environment" notices at scenario start, scenario
  end, and on debrief screens. These are mandatory and cannot be dismissed without
  acknowledgment.
- **Forex leverage display:** Before any simulated leveraged forex position is opened,
  the Service displays a notice that leverage amplifies both profits and losses and that
  this is a practice session. This notice must be acknowledged before continuing.

## 4. Prohibited Conduct

Users may not:

(a) **Signal-selling and investment advice:** Post, share, or distribute specific
entry, exit, or position-size directives tied to any real asset, in any channel or
format. The test is content, not channel or framing. Any post containing a specific
entry/exit directive for a real asset is prohibited regardless of where it appears.

(b) **Solicitation:** Solicit or recruit other users for any external trading service,
managed-account offering, paid signal service, investment pool, or trading scheme.

(c) **Impersonation:** Impersonate any coach, moderator, staff member, or official
TradeGame account. Coaches and staff never initiate direct messages to users; any
unsolicited direct message claiming to be from TradeGame is a scam.

(d) **Manipulation coordination:** Coordinate or attempt to coordinate any pump-and-
dump, short squeeze, or other asset-price manipulation through any TradeGame channel
or platform.

(e) **Personal monetization by coaches:** Coach volunteers may not solicit or accept
personal compensation from users in any form — monetary payments, tips, in-kind
gifts of material value, or any other economic benefit — in connection with their
coaching activities.

(f) **Deceptive earnings claims:** Post or share trading results in a manner designed
to imply that similar results are typical or attainable. Member wins may be shared
with appropriate typicality disclosures consistent with FTC Endorsement Guides
requirements (RISK_REGISTER §17), but not as implied earnings claims.

(g) **Exploitation of minors:** Engage minors in coaching channels, attempt to bypass
the age screen, or direct financial content to users who have identified as under 18.

(h) General: Violate applicable law, harass other users, or attempt to circumvent any
technical control implemented by the Service.

Violations of (a) through (d) may result in immediate removal and ban. The Service
reserves the right to remove content and accounts at any time for violations of these
Terms.

## 5. User-Generated Content — License and Takedown

**License:** By posting content to the Service (including journal entries, community
posts, replay annotations, and similar), users grant TradeGame a non-exclusive,
royalty-free license to display and distribute that content within the Service for
the purposes of operating the community and coaching programs.

**Journal entries:** Journal entries are stored encrypted at rest and are never
included in shared replay logs. See the Privacy Policy for details.

**Takedown — advice content:** TradeGame may remove any content that constitutes or
appears to constitute a specific trading recommendation, investment advice, signal, or
personalized financial guidance, regardless of the channel in which it appears. This
is a content-based rule: the test is what the post says, not where it was posted.

Takedown SLA for advice-content posts flagged by moderators: 48 hours from report to
removal action (solo-founder realistic cadence). The Service will acknowledge reports
within 24 hours.

**No amplification of advice content:** TradeGame will not pin, feature, repost, or
amplify user-generated content that contains actionable trading guidance. Doing so
would convert the Service from platform to publisher with respect to that content.

## 6. Coach Volunteers

Coaches are unpaid volunteers who have agreed to a separate code of conduct that
prohibits providing specific trade advice and prohibits personal monetization.

- Coaches are not employees, contractors, or agents of TradeGame for liability purposes.
- Coaches are not registered investment advisers or commodity trading advisers. Nothing
  in a coaching session constitutes financial advice.
- Coaching content is scoped to process: market structure literacy, trade-plan
  construction, journaling practice, and risk-management discipline.
- Cohort calls, office hours, and AMAs are either recorded (with participant notice) or
  conducted with at least two organization-affiliated participants present. No solo
