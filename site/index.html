What We Cover

Three markets. All treated as first-class.

Crypto

Spot trading, day trading, grid strategies, DCA, liquidity pools. We also cover how crypto intersects with gaming — because that overlap is real and worth understanding clearly.

Stocks

Long-term investing, ETFs, swing trading, earnings seasons. Slow and boring is a valid strategy. We teach it without apologizing for it.

Forex

Major pairs, session timing, and — above everything — leverage danger. Forex is where new traders blow up fast. We make risk management the first lesson, not the last.

The Game

TradeGame — education video game Sim only / no real money

TradeGame is an education video game — a trading-practice simulator. None of it touches real money. That is the point.

  • Paper-trading sandboxes Practice entries, exits, and sizing in a live-feed sim. Crypto, stocks, and forex each have their own sandbox. Mess up freely; nothing real is at stake.
  • Scenario replays Step into historical market moments tick-by-tick. Make your calls. Then get the debrief — what happened, why, and what the decision looked like in real time. It is the equivalent of watching your VOD with a coach.
  • Strategy sandboxes A visual crypto grid-bot builder that shows where the strategy breaks. DCA and rebalancing sims for stocks. Session-timing sims for forex. Build it, watch it fail, understand it better.
  • Risk-management drills Position-sizing puzzles. Drawdown-survival challenges. A "blow the account on purpose" teaching mode that makes the lesson vivid without costing you anything real.
  • Progression that rewards process XP comes from sizing correctly, honoring your stops, and journaling — not from getting lucky on a trade. A good process with a bad outcome gets celebrated as a lesson. A lucky outcome with bad process earns you nothing.

The Community

TradeGame runs on Discord.

Channels are organized by market pillar — learn, analysis, strategy, and process journals — where losses are posted alongside everything else. That is not optional; it is the culture.

Coaching ladder — earned, not bought

The ladder goes: Learner → Helper → Coach. You move up by completing game progression — not by paying a subscription tier. Coaches here teach process, risk management, and trade journaling. They do not call trades.

Replay reviews

Coaches annotate your sim trades the same way a game coach reviews your recorded matches. You see exactly where your thinking broke down — in a sim, before it costs anything real.

Peer cohorts

Learn alongside people at the same level. Every cohort covers all three pillars so nobody siloes into only crypto or only stocks.

The Lines We Don't Cross

This section is a feature, not a disclaimer.

These are the things we structurally refuse to do, and we think you should know them before you join.

  • No financial advice — ever.

    Nobody in TradeGame tells you what to buy or sell. Not coaches, not staff, not the game. If you are looking for someone to tell you what to trade, this is not the right place.

  • No signals.

    No buy/sell calls, no "VIP alpha" tiers, no insider channels. Signal-selling is the pattern we define ourselves against. The community is built so it cannot exist here.

  • No real money in the game.

    The simulator never connects to a real brokerage or exchange account. Simulated results are not real trading. A win in the game means you practiced well — that is all.

  • No gambling mechanics.

    No loot boxes. No wager loops. No features that reward chance over process. Progression here is earned by doing the boring things correctly.

FAQ

Questions

  • Will you tell me what to buy?

    No. Not ever. That is the one thing TradeGame is explicitly built not to do. If that is what you need, a licensed financial advisor is the right call.

  • Is this financial advice?

    No. TradeGame is education only. Nothing on this site, in the Discord, in the game, or from any coach or community member constitutes financial advice. Trading involves risk of significant loss. Consult a licensed financial professional before trading with real money.

  • Can I lose money here?

    Not in the game — the simulator uses no real money. In real markets, yes, you absolutely can lose money, including more than you put in if leverage is involved. That is exactly why we practice in a sim first.

  • How is the coaching ladder different from buying a course?

    You do not pay to climb it. You earn your place by completing game progression — drills, replays, journals. Coaches are community members who proved their process, not vendors selling access.

  • Is it free?

    The Discord community and the coaching ladder are community-run. The game is in development. Pricing details will be confirmed once the game ships; nothing is decided yet.

  • Do I need to know how to trade already?

    No. Learner is the bottom rung of the ladder for a reason. The game starts with risk management basics because that is what most people skip and most regret skipping.

  • Is this for a specific age group?

    TradeGame is intended for adults 18 and older. Markets involve real financial risk and we do not design content for minors.

  • What markets does the game cover?

    Crypto, stocks, and forex — all three treated as first-class. No pillar is a bolt-on. Scenarios, drills, and sandbox tools exist across all three.
